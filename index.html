Relax, Refresh, Recharge and Repeat

Hlalanathi B&B is a romantic destination on the edge of the Atlantic Ocean. It is ideal for anything from a luxury beach holiday to a weekend breakaway or a honeymoon. This luxury bed and breakfast near Cape Town is a whale watching haven nestled away in the tranquil Pringle Bay nature conservation. It is less than an hour drive from CT International Airport and boasts with world class beaches, hiking trails, surf spots and a vast selection of outdoor activities. This newly refurbished accommodation has seven modern sea facing bedrooms as well as a two bedroom ocean “Residence” with unbeatable sea views.

Housekeeping

Our goal is to provide a superior service with open communication while building a long term relationship with our customers. Ring a bell for our housekeeping service and we will gladly clean your room. It is our goal to clean your room to your satisfaction.

Room Security

It is our priority to protect our guests. We offer a number of safety and security measures like Card Locks: Guest room locking systems these days include punch and magnetic key cards Security Guards: Trained security guards working 24-hours every day to provide the best in safety and security for the guests. Security Cameras: Security cameras with digital technology. Fire Alarms: Smoke detectors and fire alarms in each guest room. Emergency Power: Provision for emergency power in case of a power cut to provide uninterrupted guest service.
